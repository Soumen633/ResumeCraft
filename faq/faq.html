Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a resume using Resume Craft?

Creating a resume with Resume Craft is easy! Simply sign up for an account, choose a template, and follow our step-by-step guide to fill in your information. Our intuitive interface and pre-written content suggestions make the process quick and effortless.

Is Resume Craft free to use?

Yes, Resume Craft offers a free basic plan that allows you to create and download a resume. We also offer premium plans with additional features, templates, and career resources for those who want to take their job search to the next level.

Are Resume Craft templates ATS-friendly?

Absolutely! All of our templates are designed to be ATS (Applicant Tracking System) friendly. This means they use simple, clean layouts and standard fonts that can be easily read by both humans and computer systems, increasing your chances of getting past initial screenings.

Can I customize the design of my resume?

Yes, you can customize your resume design with Resume Craft. Our templates offer various color schemes and layout options. Premium users have access to more advanced customization features, allowing you to create a unique resume that reflects your personal brand while remaining professional.

How often can I update my resume?

You can update your resume as often as you like! We encourage users to keep their resumes up-to-date with their latest experiences and skills. Your resume is saved in your account, allowing you to make changes and generate new versions whenever needed.

Cookie Policy

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience and allow certain features to function properly.

2. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for various purposes, including:

  • To keep you signed in
  • To remember your preferences
  • To understand how you use our website
  • To improve our services and user experience

3. Types of Cookies We Use

We use both session cookies (which expire when you close your browser) and persistent cookies (which stay on your device for a set period or until you delete them).

4. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are placed by third-party services that appear on our pages. We use these to provide features like analytics and social media integration.

5. Managing Cookies

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. However, limiting cookies may impact your experience of our website.

6. Changes to This Policy
